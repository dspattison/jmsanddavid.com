Location

The Lodge at Sugar Bowl sleeps up to 80 people in 27 rooms. Our Bridal Suite offers panoramic views of both Mt. Lincoln and Mt. Disney, access to a sundeck, quaint living areas and a spacious bathroom with separate dressing facilities. Our guest rooms can accommodate 2-6 people with views of the mountain or gondola.

We are happy to book rooms on a first come first serve basis or the bride and groom may also assign rooms to friends and family.

Rate:

  • $135.00 per night, double occupancy
  • $35.00 per extra guest

Check in: 3:00 p.m. Friday or day of arrival. A 7:00 a.m. check-in will be available for your guests on the day of your wedding.

Check out: 11:00 a.m. Sunday. Check out time may be extended for bride and groom on this day upon availability.
